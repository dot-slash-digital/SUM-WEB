About Us

Hiring a safe and experienced excavation contractor is the most important initial decision a general contractor will make. It sets the precedence for scheduling, safety, project organization and jobsite appearance. The experience and knowledge you receive from an experienced excavation contractor can save time, money, and unnecessary delays. Summex Excavating LLC has over 30 years experience in the excavation and utility field.
